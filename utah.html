Our Process

Giving your landscape a makeover can feel like a daunting task, but we’re here to break it down into a few easy steps!

1

Explore what incentives and programs are available in your area, Utah has a lot to offer. If you’re lost, Utah Water Savers is a great place to start!

2

Apply to one of these programs, they’ll determine if your lawn is eligible for any rebate or other incentive. There are a lot out there so apply away.

3

While you wait you can start designing and planning your own garden by downloading our easy to use Edible Landscape app.

4

Once you know if you’ve been accepted for any program or incentive you can checkout on the app and bring your hard work into reality!

5

Receive all the materials and plants for your new Edible Landscape and create the garden of your dreams! The final step is to enjoy your new way of life!
