Landscape Conversion (Turf Replacement) Rebate

Program Details

The converted landscape area must have 30% coverage with living, desert-adapted plants, either new or existing. The expected mature size of plants can count towards this coverage. Existing landscape plants that are not desert-adapted may be used to calculate the 30% mature plant coverage. Soil must be covered with rock, or organic mulch. Bare soil landscapes will not be accepted, nor will artificial turf landscapes that do not incorporate at least 30% cover with living, desert-adapted plants, either new or existing.

Rebate Details

Approved applications will provide a rebate of $0.25 per square foot of grass removed and replaced with desert landscaping, capped at $2,000 per residential property.
