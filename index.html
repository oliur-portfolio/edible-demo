Frequently asked questions

FAQ

We’re here to answer all your inquiries! Drop us a message via email if we’re still not on the same page.

What is Edible Landscapes, and how can it help me turn my lawn into an edible garden?

Edible Landscapes is an innovative gardening app that empowers you to transform your lawn into a productive and beautiful edible garden, all for as little as $99.

How can I reach out to Edible Landscapes for support or assistance?

You can contact our support team via email at feedback@ediblelandscapes.com. We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.

Is Edible Landscapes compatible with both iOS and Android devices?

Yes, Edible Landscapes is available for both iOS and Android devices, ensuring that you can access it on your preferred platform.

What does "Custom Garden Design" involve in Edible Landscapes, and how is it tailored to my unique needs?

Our "Custom Garden Design" service provides you with a personalized landscape design that considers your specific needs, space, climate, and lifestyle. We even include a single revision to ensure your garden design aligns perfectly with your preferences.

How does Edible Landscapes ensure the security and privacy of user data?

We take data privacy and security seriously. For detailed information, please read our terms of use, where we explain how we protect your data.

What are the initial steps for downloading and getting started with the Edible Landscapes mobile app?

To get started, simply download the Edible Landscapes mobile app from the App Store (iOS). Follow the easy onboarding process to set up your account and begin using the app.

What resources does Edible Landscapes provide to help me with garden maintenance?

We don't have garden maintenance features in our app at the moment, but we're excited to let you know that they are coming soon. We're continually working to enhance your gardening experience, and you can look forward to future updates that will include helpful garden maintenance tools and resources.

How many revisions can I expect during the custom garden design process?

You can expect a single revision as part of the custom garden design process. Our dedicated landscapers are committed to ensuring your satisfaction. We have a simple and easy-to-use feedback system that allows you to provide your input, so you can get the results you want. Your feedback is valuable in creating a garden design that aligns perfectly with your preferences.

What payment options are available for Edible Landscapes' services?

We accept various payment methods, including all major credit cards, Google and Apple pay. You can choose the option that best suits your needs during the checkout process.
